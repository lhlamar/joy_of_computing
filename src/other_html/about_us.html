What is this?

This is a site that we made for our COMP5620 semester project. The goal of this project was to create an interactive learning platform to allow students interested in Computer Science to learn some new concepts in a new, informative, and fun sort of way.

How do I use this?

This site was designed with the intent of being intuitive and easy to use, however it's to be expected that maybe that's not the case for everyone, and that's okay! To get started, you can select from either the home page, or from the navigation at the top of the screen, one of the lessons. Once you have chosen your lesson, read through the article and then check out the interactive activity at the end of each lesson to test and solidify your understanding of the topic.

Who are we?

Our names are Lucas Lamar, Jessica Bozkho, and Liz Chatel. We are on the Joy and Beauty of Computing team for our class and have designed and implemented this site for use of new CS students.
